Server Rules

As is Customary for any Discord Server, we've gotta have a Set of Rules to keep things in check. Please take a Read over them before Hopping into TheRabbitHole Discord. These Rules should be Common Sense & shouldn't take too much thought, but they're still important none the less.

Follow Discord TOS Unconditionally on All Discord Servers.
Listen to Staff - In The End they've got the Final Say & Judgement on Things.
Be Unconditionally Respectful to All Users & Mods
No Use of Slurs or Derogatory Terms - Especially Against Other Users)
No Expression of Bigoted Beliefs/Opinions or Bigotry against Other Users
No Spamming of Any Kind - This Especially Pertains to The Art Chat
Keep Server Content Appropriate - Preferably No Politics

If you've got any Questions or Concerns about any of this, then head over to the Ask Me Anything Chat. That also goes for if you'd like to report anything amiss.
