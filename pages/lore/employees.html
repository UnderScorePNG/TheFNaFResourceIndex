Channel Management

It's a bit tricky to understand how exactly each Client Company interacts with the others in order for The Fazbear Funtime Service to function properly, however odds are the System looks something like this. The Behavior of The Fazbear Funtime Service Animatronics is dictated by a System developed by The Client Company which is no longer a part of the Service. Since that Company was let go from the Project, a group of Employees at Fazbear Entertainment have been responsible for designing the Services New Animatronics. These new Animatronics & Their Parts are Shipped to the Distribution Warehouse by DLZ Shipping Solutions. The Distribution Warehouse is responsible for Housing, Assembling, Repairing, and Distributing all Funtime Service Delivery Animatronics.
